Ray relaying

Contributor: Stas Fainer

This is a simulation of ray relaying by a series of ideal identical lenses with focal length \(f\) and distance \(d\) between consecutive lenses. A non-divergent ray trajectory is guaranteed if and only if \(d\leq 4f\). The script for creating these series of lenses is available here.
