Plano-concave resonator

Contributor: Mikhail Kochiev

This is a demonstration of a plano-concave resonator. If the distance between the mirrors is greater than the radius of curvature, the resonator is unstable. If the distance between the mirrors is less than or equal to the radius of curvature, a stable resonator mode is formed.
