Hyperbolischer Spiegel

Contributor: Stas Fainer

This is a demonstration of the reflection property of the hyperbolic mirror: Light rays traveling from one focus towards the hyperbolic mirror, will reflect away from the other focus.

The two foci of the hyperbola are aplanatic points (see Aplanatic points).
