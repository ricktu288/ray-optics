បង្កើតដោយ៖ Yi-Ting Tu

This simulation shows the images formed by two mirrors. The green "L" is the object. If the angle between the two mirrors is \(\frac{360^\circ}{n}\), then \(n-1\) virtual images (the orange L's) will be formed. You can drag the endpoint of the mirror to change the angle between them.
